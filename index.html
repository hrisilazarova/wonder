SUMMER CAMP MADE FUN

A summer at Camp Wonder Journey means spending your summer hiking, backpacking, horseback riding, and playing in the beautiful Rocky Mountains. Wake up each morning to freshmountain air, views of peaks that soar to 14,000 feet, and the laughter of your fellow campers. Since 1985, Camp Wonder Journey has welcomed more than 10,000 young people from every state and more than a dozen countries, including many second, third and now fourth generation campers.
